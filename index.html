About SolTeq

SolTeq is a tech startup that utilizes artificial intelligence to simplify intricate tasks and streamline everyday processes for everyday people, making them more efficient and user-friendly.
SolTeq's first priority is to tacklee the travel and tourism industry by creating AI-powered travel designed to simplify the traveling process.

How are we Different?

The travel industry faces several challenges, including the overwhelming amount of information available for travelers to sift through. With numerous applications and sites to consider, planning a vacation can be stressful and time-consuming, often leading to travelers feeling like they need a break from their break. To address these issues, TravelMás.AI aims to take the hassle out of travel planning and logistics by using AI to provide personalized recommendations and optimize the travel experience for each individual. Through crowdsourcing data, AI can analyze customer preferences, automate repetitive tasks, improve operational efficiency, ensure safety and security, and optimize pricing and inventory strategies. A survey conducted by TravelMás.AI revealed that budget is not the top concern for travelers, but rather the desire to optimize their time, fulfill their bucket list, and enjoy good food. TravelMás.AI's goal is to create a stress-free and personalized travel experience for customers, focusing on their needs and wants rather than advertising-based revenue models.
